How Plants Make Food

Plants are amazing because they can make their own food through a process called photosynthesis. Here's how it works:

  • Plants capture sunlight using chlorophyll in their leaves
  • They take in carbon dioxide from the air through tiny holes
  • Water is absorbed through their roots
  • Sunlight energy helps combine water and carbon dioxide to make sugar
  • Oxygen is released into the air as a byproduct
