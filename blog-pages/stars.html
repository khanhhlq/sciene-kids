How Do Stars Shine?

Stars shine through a process called nuclear fusion. Here's how it works:

  • Hydrogen atoms in the star's core combine under extreme pressure and temperature
  • This fusion creates helium and releases enormous energy
  • The energy travels to the star's surface as light and heat
  • This light travels through space and reaches our eyes
