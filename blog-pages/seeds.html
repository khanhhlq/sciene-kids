From Seed to Plant

Every plant starts its life as a tiny seed. Let's discover the amazing journey of how a seed grows into a plant:

  • Seeds need water to wake up (germination)
  • First, a tiny root grows down into the soil
  • Then, a shoot pushes up through the soil towards sunlight
  • Leaves develop to make food through photosynthesis
  • The plant continues to grow bigger and stronger
