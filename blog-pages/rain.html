What Makes Rain?

Rain is part of the amazing water cycle. Let's explore how raindrops form:

  • The sun heats up water from oceans, lakes, and rivers
  • Water turns into invisible water vapor (evaporation)
  • Water vapor rises and cools in the sky
  • Tiny water droplets form clouds (condensation)
  • When droplets get too heavy, they fall as rain
