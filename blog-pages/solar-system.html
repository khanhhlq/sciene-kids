Our Solar System

Our solar system is an amazing family of planets orbiting around our Sun. Let's explore each planet:

  • Mercury - The smallest and closest planet to the Sun
  • Venus - The hottest planet with thick clouds
  • Earth - Our home planet with water and life
  • Mars - The red planet with huge volcanoes
  • Jupiter - The largest planet with a big red spot
  • Saturn - The planet with beautiful rings
  • Uranus - The sideways planet that rolls like a ball
  • Neptune - The windy blue planet
